Classroom Demonstrations

These demonstrations are available for use in your classroom. Please let us know if you would like to borrow them or would like your visiting physicist to bring them along when he/she visits your class.

  • Observe cosmic rays in the Cloud Chamber.
  • Measure the Speed of Light using a laser.
  • Determine the ratio of charge to mass of on electron using the e/m Apparatus.
  • Learn how physicists detect particles using marbles.
