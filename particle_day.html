Introduction

PARTICLE Day is a research conference at the University of Rochester for teachers and students participating in the PARTICLE program. This is an opportunity for students and teachers to gather and share results from experiments using cosmic ray telescopes.

Students are also taken on tours of University of Rochester physics laboratories.
